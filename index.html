OUR STORY

“I grew up playing mahjong in Singapore, in a home ruled by my formidable grandmother, Dolly. But it was never just about the game. My memories are of balmy evenings, the air thick with the scent of tropical fruit from the garden, mingling with the rich, spicy aromas of Dolly’s Peranakan cooking. Laughter and lively chatter spilled through the house as friends gathered around the mahjong table, the rhythmic clatter of tiles a steady backdrop to gossip shared and stories retold. It was a feeling as much as a memory—of warmth, of belonging, of time slowing down in the company of others.
Now, in NYC, a place I have come to call home, I want to recreate that same magic of bringing people together through mahjong.”

-Subhas Kim Kandasamy, Founder of Mahjong Palace.

ABOUT MJP

Founded in NYC in September 2024, Mahjong Palace (MJP) is a social mahjong club that reimagines the mahjong experience. We host engaging teaching and playing sessions at thoughtfully selected venues - from art galleries and cultural spaces to restaurants and members-only clubs.

Our booking system is simple and straightforward - tickets can be purchased directly on our webstore and guarantee a seat at a table. A drinks bar and a smorgasboard of Asian snacks and appetizers is offered at each event, along with a curated music playlist that is available for download after the session on Spotify.

Our events are designed to cater to mahjong players of all levels.

We teach and play Hong Kong–style mahjong, the most widely played version of the game worldwide.


TEACHING TABLE: Perfect for absolute beginners, Teaching Tables are led by experienced teachers. New players are welcome to book sessions at Teaching Tables as many times as needed to feel confident with the rules and structure of the game.

OPEN PLAY: Players who are at least comfortable playing Chicken Hand independently are welcome to book a seat at an Open Play Table. We aim to group participants with others at a similar skill level to ensure a balanced and enjoyable game.
